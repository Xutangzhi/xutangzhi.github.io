# Tangzhi Xu

### Bio
I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Softwiser Group at the Institute of Computer Software, Nanjing University, supervised by Assoc. Prof. Yuan Yao and Prof. Feng Xu.  
My research focuses on **Large Language Models for Software Engineering**, with particular emphasis on **automation and software quality**, including test case updating and program repair. I am currently continuing research on **test generation**.
