Bio

我目前是南京大学计算机软件研究所 Softwiser Group 的博士三年级在读生，导师为姚远副教授与徐锋教授。 我的研究方向聚焦于面向软件工程的大语言模型（LLM for Software Engineering），重点关注自动化与软件质量保障， 包括测试用例更新、代码修复等问题。目前仍持续关注测试生成相关研究。

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Softwiser Group at the Institute of Computer Software, Nanjing University, supervised by Assoc. Prof. Yuan Yao and Prof. Feng Xu. My research focuses on Large Language Models for Software Engineering, with emphasis on automation and software quality, including test case updating and program repair. I am currently continuing research on test generation.
